The Elect is a one-person lab making small local voice models — fine-tuned 7B experiments built around distinct registers, personas, and sensibilities. This is the umbrella page for the fleet — Reformation prophets, register oracles, and the radical voices of history (most recently the women of revolution — Emma Goldman, Rosa Luxemburg, Louise Michel, Joan of Arc — a Gnostic divine-voice oracle (Abraxas), a dual-mode Clausewitz, and the radical historical Jesus (den-of-thieves)). Each model is a standalone artifact. Most ship as Ollama-compatible GGUF files; a few are withheld register studies — held back for copyright or a protective fanbase (Mark E. Smith, Captain Beefheart, and Simone Weil / decreation) — present on the roster with a showcase page but no public weights. The live roster: https://lerugray.github.io/the-elect/
